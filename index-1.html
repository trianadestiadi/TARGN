Our History

Born to Ride, United to Inspire

Team Arogan was founded by a group of young individuals who shared a passion for riding motorcycles, particularly large-engine bikes. The group quickly gained attention for their distinctive style and strong solidarity, creating a unique community among motorcycle enthusiasts.

Merchandise with a Mission

Recognizing their growing popularity, Team Arogan launched an official merchandise line. Their products not only represented the group’s identity but also inspired many people by promoting a positive perception of the large-engine motorcycle community.

Evolving Beyond Limits

Team Arogan continued to grow and mature, improving in community quality, products, and influence. Their innovation and dedication established the team as an icon in the moge world, gaining an ever-expanding fanbase.

Accelerating into the Future

In 2024, Team Arogan maintained consistent growth, focusing on enhancing quality across all aspects. They solidified their position as one of the most influential and inspiring communities in the world of large-engine motorcycles.
